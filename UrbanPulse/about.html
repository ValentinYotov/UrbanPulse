About the Project

Urban Pulse provides an innovative platform for the citizens of Sofia, allowing them to report hazards and incidents in real-time, thus informing the community about potential risks in the city. The platform promotes the improvement of public safety by collecting, analyzing, and presenting reliable information in a convenient and interactive way, facilitating effective decision-making and the overall well-being of citizens.

In the Near Future

  • Adding user profiles and personalized notification options.
  • Improving statistics with dynamic graphs and real-time analysis.
  • Interactive map with heatmap functionality for visualizing areas with the most incidents.
  • Adding an AI chatbot for user questions and assistance.
  • Gamification with rewards and badges for active users.
  • Adding a centralized database that will connect all reports and make the system more efficient for the Sofia community.
